About

I’m Chris Yerikian, based in Southern California. I’m co-CEO of Surje Coffee Co., and I also work in safety and risk. I’m CSP-certified and spend a lot of time turning messy operations into clear systems that actually work.

On the creative side, I write and develop film projects, including a feature screenplay set during the early pandemic. I’m also deeply interested in Bitcoin, Lightning, and building products that are useful, durable, and hard to copy.
